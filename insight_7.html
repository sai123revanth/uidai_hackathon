Identity Anxiety

Socio-Economic Forensic Module
The Core Philosophy

How a citizen interacts with their ID reveals their stability.
High anxiety leads to frantic corrections; stability leads to natural updates.

The DBDI Algorithm

DBDI =
Demographic Corrections (Friction) Biometric Updates (Compliance)
Numerator (Top) Voluntary fixes (Name, DOB). High numbers mean rejection at banks/jobs.
Denominator (Bottom) Mandatory rules (Age 5/15). Just following orders, not using the ID.

Cluster Definitions

> 2.5
Hyper-Correction (Anxiety) Frantic updates. Often seen in border areas or migration hubs. People struggling to prove identity.
< 0.2
Digital Dormancy ID sits in a drawer. Updates happen only when forced by rules. Low economic usage.
0.2 - 2.5
Balanced Economy Healthy state. Updates happen naturally alongside mandatory refreshes.
